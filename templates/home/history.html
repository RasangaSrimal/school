History

March of Time

{% blocktranslate %} A dire need then prevailed for a school in North Kanumuladeniya of North Giruwa Pattu in Hambantota District at about 1928. Thereupon Mr. M.D. Sadiris and Mr. M.T. Singho ; the founding fathers" the two distinguished personalities then lived in the area with the constant support and assistance of the villagers were able to pitch a coconut thatched shed that coincides with the location of the present school was declared open by bringing in a paid teacher from Deiyandara to thus set up a school with 5 children. As the time went by, the need for a new building gradually emerged. So that the new building together with a teacher's living quarters built through the good offices of donors and the then officials in the area was officially transferred to the government on 21 st January 1933. Subsequently, the teaching and learning process got off the ground with seven (7) children in July 1933. It was recorded that Mr. D.D.N. Kulasekara had held the office of principal first. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} There were classes from grade one to four in the beginning. There was a Sunday school and an adult class in addition to teaching medicine, English, Pali and Sanskrit to juniors in this school from 1933 to 1953 Subquently, the children who got through the junior level exam were facilitated to further their education in senior grades on 1 st of January 1954 removing the longstanding adversities and obstacles the students faced thus far.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} It was reported that nine (9) students out of the total had passed the senior exam for the first time according to the historical information available in the log books. In addition, a volleyball team that beat their rivals at both divisional and provincial levels was able to represent the school at the all island volleyball tournament held in 1956.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Thereafter the then chief incumbent of Chandimarama temple ; late venerable Chandima Thera with some villagers were instrumental in upgrading the school to Maha Vidyalaya status. As a result of their relentless pursuit; the school was recognized as H/Kanumuldeniya North Maha Viddyalaya on 24 th December 1964.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Mr. Gunapala Welhenage currently a resident in UK and Mr. U. M. Gunapala who had later become a lawyer and an assistant commissioner of labour respectively were the first to gain the privilege of university admission from Kanumuldeniya School.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} This school has been able to produce eminent personalities both in academic and sports levels. In this regard winning the Beliatta Division challenge trophy on 29 March 1963 under 16 drill display team all island participation on 13th July 1963 and winning the Mulgirigala Division competition were to name but a few. {% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Later in 1980 receiving a library building which was lacking for ages and then school being promoted to 1AB type in 1986 marked a turning point in the history of the school. When looking back on the history, Mr. M.D. Nagarathna deserves a special mention as he was an asset to our school. He received his basic education by being under roof of his alma-mater North Kanumuldeniya. Later he became an obstetrician (gynecologist){% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} On top of everything; this school has been able to produce many a young student who excelled in medicine, engineering and other streams as well in the school history. A new chapter dawned in the school's history was that the concept of introducing an Old Boys Association which was once limited to a few leading schools in cities and suburbs. Later it gradually became a reality in 1999 with the first annual prize giving ceremony taking place. This was in fact, as a result of a long drawn up plan implemented by a few astute old boys living in Colombo and Embilipitiya. The uninterrupted, continuous prize giving ceremony that has been held annually with the strenuous generous financial support and guidance of all stakeholders involved would undoubtedly create a profound positive effect on the school environment paving the way for the children to excel in different disciplines since its inception.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} Further; holding the annual prize giving and spending a whole hoard of money was by no means a waste, as it is obvious by the fact that the percentage of G.C.E (O/L) and G.C.E (A/L) exams results have been on the increase year by year. Our school that was developing slowly though, became a Navodya school and later it came under the concept of secondary school status thus finally being recommended as a National school on its way to success.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} The present â€“ eye- catching surroundings of the school made up of perfect combination of human and physical resources, fascinating shrine room and drinking water project were literally due to overcoming numerous obstacles and challenges to contribute to add peace and tranquility to the mind still evoking a feeling of appeal and victory looking back on the past on its way to success. While reflecting on the teaching and listening process; it transpires that the bilingual unit together with other advanced level streams blends a perfect learner friendly atmosphere in this school to be comprehensively competitive and victorious over fraternal schools in the zone day by day. And also many children have been able to show their talents and skills in extracurricular activities at national level too.{% endblocktranslate %}

{% blocktranslate %} A school commenced with a coconut thatched shed has a long history is made popular everywhere right from the beginning by many, among whom the venerable Bikku (Monks) and the chief prelate of Chandimarama temple venerable Chandima Thera who acted in an advisory capacity, principals with staff, past pupils, distinguished members, officials, and villagers were commended and commemorated. And also, the chief priests as well as other monks, laymen, past pupils, distinguished persons and especially the forerunner, current principal, Mr. H. Chandrasiri are all respectively and fondly remembered again for their invaluable services, rendered to launch the school on its trajectory. Thus is a school that triumphed over a numerous adversities for nearly a period of century has become a National school since â€¦â€¦â€¦. April 2021. People from all walks of life aspire and sincerely wish that their school may flourish by leaps and bounds to produce a bunch of people who are worthy and useful citizens to the world.{% endblocktranslate %}
